Business Context

Built around Funngro's real value proposition

Funngro connects teenagers with companies so they can work on real projects, gain early professional exposure, and earn money before turning 18. This redesign keeps that mission intact while making the product feel more credible, premium, and future-facing.

Teen aspiration

Clear growth path, stronger confidence messaging, and motivational microcopy tailored to ambitious students.

Trust building

Social proof, structured service explanations, and visual hierarchy to help parents and companies trust the platform faster.

Startup polish

Product-led landing page design inspired by top SaaS brands, with smoother flows and stronger calls-to-action.

Design Direction

Visual principles behind the redesign

Bold gradients

Atmospheric backgrounds and layered lighting create a premium startup feel without overwhelming readability.

Glassmorphism panels

Soft translucent surfaces add depth, clarity, and a more elevated sense of digital craftsmanship.

Interactive motion

Scroll reveals, magnetic buttons, parallax drift, and hover transitions make the experience feel alive.
